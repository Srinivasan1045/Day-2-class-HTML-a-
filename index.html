Spartans XI Cricket Club is a passionate and energetic cricket team built on unity, discipline, and a love for the game. Lead by Srinivasan, our mission is to bring players together, compete with spirit, and continuously grow as a team. Whether on turf wicket or matting wicket, Spartans XI plays with heart, respect, and unstoppable energy. Every match is not just a game — it's a battle we face like true Spartans!


Spartans XI Cricket Club is more than just a team — it's a brotherhood built on passion, discipline, and the spirit of the Game.We believe in playing bold, respecting the game, and giving our best in every match. Whether it's a friendly game or a tournament battle, we bring the same fire, focus, and unity to the field.
